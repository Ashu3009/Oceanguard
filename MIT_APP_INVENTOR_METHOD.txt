TROUBLESHOOTING:
===============================================================

Problem: Can't login
Solution: Use Gmail account, allow pop-ups

Problem: APK download not starting
Solution: Disable ad blocker, try again

Problem: App crashes on phone
Solution: Check WebViewer is added correctly

Problem: Gallery not loading
Solution: Check URL has http:// at start
